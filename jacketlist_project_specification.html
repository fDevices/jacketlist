# JacketList — Project Specification ## Project Overview **JacketList** (jacketlist.com) is a book discovery website with two core features: 1. **Weekly Bestsellers** — ranked list aggregated from NYT, The Guardian, and Goodreads 2. **Book Series Guide** — reading order (chronological + author's recommended) for popular series across all genres Revenue comes from **Amazon Associates affiliate links** on every book, and a **footer ad zone** supporting Google AdSense, BookBub, and direct sponsor placements. --- ## Styling Rules - **Color palette:** warm neutrals (cream/off-white background), deep navy or charcoal for text, amber/gold accents - **Typography:** Serif font for titles (Georgia or similar), sans-serif for UI - **Book cards:** White background, subtle shadow, rounded corners — clean and editorial - **Layout:** Mobile-first responsive grid: 1 col (mobile) → 2 col (tablet) → 3–4 col (desktop) --- ## Core Components - **BookCard:** Shows cover, title, author, score badge (🔥/⬆️/👀), source badges, "weeks on list" if > 1. Includes "Buy on Amazon" button. - **SeriesCard:** Overview for homepage grid. - **AdCard:** Visually matches BookCard exactly with a "Sponsored" label. - **ReadingOrderTabs:** Toggle between "📖 Chronological" and "✍️ Author's Recommended". - **FooterAdZone:** "You might also enjoy" section with 1-3 AdCards. --- ## Pages to Design 1. **HomePage (`/`):** Hero (tagline + search), Weekly Bestsellers, Popular Series grid, Footer Ad Zone. 2. **SeriesPage (`/series/:id`):** Series header, bestseller banner (if applicable), ReadingOrderTabs, Footer Ad Zone.
